Our Heritage

The Story of Bou Thakurani Banquet

Founded on the principle of bringing regal luxury to social events in Kolkata, we have built a reputation for excellence.

The name "Bou Thakurani" is inspired by traditional Bengali royalty and warmth. Established with the vision of offering a premium multi-purpose banqueting space, we combine traditional hospitality with state-of-the-art infrastructure. From high-quality central air conditioning to premium stages decorated with exotic flowers, every element has been curated to make our hosts feel proud.

Over the years, we have hosted hundreds of weddings, traditional Annaprashan (rice ceremonies), grand receptions, birthday parties, and prestigious corporate seminars. Our professional management crew takes complete charge of lighting, seating, generator operations, and catering logistics, leaving you free to enjoy your special moments.

Milestones

Our Journey

2018

Grand Foundation

Bou Thakurani Banquet was inaugurated in Madhyamgram, Kolkata with a single large hall and basic decoration facilities, aimed at redefining local celebratory experiences.

2020

Full Central Air Conditioning

Upgraded our ventilation and cooling systems to full central air conditioning, ensuring maximum comfort for up to 1,000 guests during hot summer months.

2022

Exclusive Catering Partnership

Signed exclusive partnerships with award-winning traditional and modern Bengali/Chinese catering groups to offer premium dining solutions to clients.

2025

500+ Successful Events Landmark

Celebrated our milestone of hosting more than 500 successful social, corporate, and traditional ritual events with record customer feedback.
